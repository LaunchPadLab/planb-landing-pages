About Us

eLEND is a division of American Financial Resources, Inc. We are a national residential mortgage lender, dedicated to helping homeowners, home buyers, and mortgage professionals achieve their financing goals by delivering innovative mortgage solutions and an unparalleled customer experience. We utilize the latest technology and deliver educational resources to our customers in an effort to simplify the mortgage process.
