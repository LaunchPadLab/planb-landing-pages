About Us

eLEND is a division of American Financial Resources, Inc. We are a national residential mortgage lender, dedicated to helping homeowners, home buyers, and mortgage professionals achieve their financing goals by delivering innovative mortgage solutions and an unparalleled customer experience. We utilize the latest technology and deliver educational resources to our customers in an effort to simplify the mortgage process.

Based in Parsippany, NJ, AFR was established in 1997, AFR is a Ginnie Mae, Fannie Mae, and Freddie Mac Seller / Servicer, FHA Mortgagee, USDA National Lender, and VA Automatic Lender. We provide Brokered and Correspondent mortgage solutions by offering an extensive program catalog with competitive pricing, unique partnership opportunities, and flexible business relationships.

We strive to exceed the expectations of our lending partners and nurture healthy, profitable relationships that provide a simplified mortgage process with the highest levels of customer service. We’re committed to providing consistent and dedicated service that meets all of our customers’ needs with a personal touch.
